Our story

Ibby Sasso has had a longtime love (read, obsession) for fragrances and the benefits that aromatherapy can bring to one’s overall well-being and mood. When Ibby and Fabio welcomed their now five year old son they were told that he was immunocompromised and would need a stem cell transplant in an effort to provide him with a fully-functioning immune system. This involved a four month stay in the hospital while their son underwent treatment and ongoing special care to this day. One way that Ibby coped and continues to manage the anxiety, stress and worry of having an immunocompromised child is through aromatherapy and taking small moments to breathe and re-center.

Fabio has never been one to sit still. Like most of the world’s population, with the onset of the pandemic he was finding himself overcome with anxiety, stress and worry. He was inspired to find a way to help himself while also encouraging others to focus on the simple act of breathing to calm the mind and reset. This resulted in the launch of Calmaria, an extremely simple breathing app that is meant to help individuals focus on their breath as a tool for improved balance and wellness.

With this as context and inspiration, Ibby and Fabio have blended their techniques to help better manage stress for mental and physical wellness by creating Terral, a small-batch, non-gendered fragrance, made with botanical ingredients and aroma molecules. This limited-edition roll-on parfum is intended for use with the breathing app, Calmaria. Functional ingredients include Hinoki, Pink Grapefruit and Black Pepper to calm the mind, Hiba Cedarwood, Lavender and Damask Rose to reduce anxiety along with Haitian Vetiver and Sandalwood to bring relaxation and peace. Flexible for layering or use on its own, Terral is beneficial to anyone needing stress-relief and tranquility while serving as a gentle reminder to breathe in and take time for yourself.

The pair partnered with L'Aromatica, a Latina-owned and operated artisan fragrance company based in Northern California on this exclusive fragrance.
